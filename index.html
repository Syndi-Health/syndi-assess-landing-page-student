Find the right mental wellbeing support, right when you need it

Syndi is a self-assessment and recommendation tool. We empower students to monitor and self-manage their mental health and wellbeing through personalised navigation to apps and resources.

GooglePlay         App Store

Features

Made simple, with lots of love

  • SMS Check-Up Reminders

    Schedule your text message prompts for re-assessments to fit into your daily life

  • Digital Clinical Questionnaires

    Easily assess your mental health and wellbeing through clinically-validated surveys

  • Progress Dashboard

    Real-time monitoring scores provides awareness and feedback of your progress

  • Personalised Recommendations

    Guidance to university resources, apps, and online support specific to your needs

  • Direct Crisis Support Links

    One-click access to 24/7 emergency helplines and chat services

  • Secure and Private Data

    Anonymised data and secure app ensures we do not share any personal data with anyone

Our Team

Meet the people behind the Syndi App

Ben Lakey

Co-Founder and CEO

Having gone through mental health care pathway here in the UK, I wanted to make sure no one feels alone and unsupported outside of, or while waiting for counselling and therapy services.

Jorge Alexander

Co-Founder and CTO

I've experienced the difficulties of mental wellbeing support at university and I'm now on a mission to use AI to support those suffering from poor mental wellbeing.

Eddy Ku

UX Designer

I have been in and out of counselling for almost 7 years now. I believe that the Syndi App will help people who are affected to find the right resources to maintain their mental health and wellbeing.
